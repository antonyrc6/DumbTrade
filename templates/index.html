    Welcome To DumbTRADE

    Welcome to DumbTrade, a cutting-edge company founded by Yasha, Quinn, Aaron, and Kush. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, user-friendly platform for novice investors to navigate the stock market. Utilizing live APIs, we offer real-time updates on stock performance, allowing our users to make informed decisions on when to buy and sell. Our platform also features interactive charts, providing a visual representation of a stock's rise and fall in value.

    DumbTrade

    Our team's expertise in web development has enabled us to create a fully-functioning website that seamlessly integrates various APIs to provide accurate and up-to-date information. We have focused on creating a user-friendly interface, making the process of investing easy and accessible to all. Our front-end development primarily consists of HTML and Javascript, ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience. Initially, our goal was to create a website that displayed the highs and lows of the stock exchange and offered insights on when to buy or sell. However, as we progressed, we recognized the complexity and technical requirements of such a task and adapted our approach to offer a more streamlined and user-friendly platform for our users. DumbTrade is committed to providing our users with the tools and information needed to succeed in the stock market, and we look forward to assisting you on your investing journey.
